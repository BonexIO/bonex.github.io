Product

Bonex gives the momentum to the emergence of a blockchain based ecosystem for loyalty bonuses management. It will serve as a one-stop solution for issuance, distribution, transfer, exchange and trading loyalty bonuses. Such an system will interlink merchants and customers across industries and countries, forming Bonex Ecosystem. It will provide tools for managing and exchanging loyalty bonuses (tokens), giving loyalty managers more flexibility and making loyalty programs more personalized.
Bonex advantages

Solution for Vendors

  • Vendors get ready technology on the blockchain with the functionality of connecting merchants.
  • Can charge profit from the merchant for connection to the platform

advantages

  • Additional income with an increasing number of clients and transactions

Solution for Merchants

  • Merchant can issue tokens
  • Tokens are exchangeable between one another

advantages

  • Better financial performance by reducing both direct and indirect costs
  • Personalization
  • Track Record

Solution for Customers

Integrated wallet Platform:
  • Access to all tokens through a single platform
  • Access to token exchange

advantages

  • Interoperability between loyalty systems
  • Integration with payment systems
  • One app for storing everything
